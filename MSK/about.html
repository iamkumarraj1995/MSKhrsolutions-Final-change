OUR RECUITMENT

Recruiting from overseas can be a challenging experience for companies. However, for our specialty is international recruitment for Singapore as well as other countries, we have the requisite experience, knowledge and network of overseas partners to ensure that the right people are recruited to join your organisation. We recruit individuals for various industries and do so at every organisational level. We keep up to date with the latest foreign manpower regulations and requirements issued by the Ministry of Manpower as well as take care of all immigration and Ministry of Manpower processing requirements to make the process hassle-free for you.

Our alliance of established, licensed recruitment partners spread across Asia can source for you manpower from

  • India
  • Bangladesh
  • China
  • Myanmar
  • Sri Lanka
  • Vietnam
  • Philippines

We regularly place professionals, technicians, skilled, semi-skilled and service personnel for the following industries

  • Construction
  • Marine
  • Hospitality
  • Healthcare
  • Transportation and logistics
  • Food and Beverage
  • Precision Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Finance & Accounting
  • Sales & Marketing

We are registered with the Ministry of Manpower's WPOL and EPOL systems for online application of work passes including

  • Work Permit
  • S Pass
  • Employment Pass
  • Dependent Pass

The range of work pass transactions that we can perform include

  • Applications
  • Issuances
  • Renewals
  • Cancellations
  • Company Quota Balance Enquiries
  • Industrial Classifications
